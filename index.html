    About Us

    Best Starch Manufacturer in Gujarat

    We manufacture High quality product for our customers

    Maruti Chemicals was established in May 1996 to manufacture Tapioca Starch and expanded with integrated manufacturing facility for Maize & Modified Starch Units along with 4.5MW Captive Power Plant in the year of 2013.

    • Quality of our Tapioca & Maize Starch (Native and Modified Starches) are at par with International Standards.
    • Quality unmatched by any other Indian starch manufacturer.
    • Well established Dealer Network all over India and exporting around 20 countries.
    • Entire Machinery / Equipment imported from Sweden, Thailand & China for our Tapioca & Maize Plants.

    We are one of the largest manufacturers of Tapioca, Maize & Modified Starches in India.
